Firebase overview I

  • Products to develop & test your app

    • Real-time database (backend for your app)
    • Authentication
    • Cloud Storage
    • Hosting
    • ...
  • Products to "grow & engage your audience"
    • Google Analytics
    • Dynamic Links
    • Remote Config
    • AdWords
    • ...
  • Tight integration with Google Cloud Platform
